Summary & Key Takeaway

The checking account appears to have regular income, but one-off expenses like travel (April) and seasonal spending (December) create sharp dips that erode balance over time. Without consistent savings, these shocks accumulate impact. Exploring month-level breakdowns and simulating different savings or expense strategies helps reveal levers to stabilize finances.
